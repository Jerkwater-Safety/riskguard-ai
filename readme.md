\fs28 \cf0 1. \uc0\u55357 \u56522  Loss Analytics (The "Vital Few")\

\f1\b0\fs24 \cf0 Automatically applies the 
\f0\b Pareto Principle
\f1\b0  to your data. It identifies the 20% of accident causes (e.g., Overexertion, Falls) that are responsible for 80% of your total incurred costs, allowing for targeted safety intervention.\

\f0\b\fs28 \cf0 2. \uc0\u55357 \u56504  Financial Leakage Engine\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa240\partightenfactor0

\f1\b0\fs24 \cf0 Quantifies the "Cost of Delay." Using actuarial correlations, this module calculates how much money is being lost due to reporting lags. It includes a 
\f0\b Scenario Simulator
\f1\b0  to show the ROI of implementing 24-hour reporting or Nurse Triage.\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa280\partightenfactor0

\f0\b\fs28 \cf0 3. \uc0\u55357 \u56521  Industry Benchmarking\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa240\partightenfactor0

\f1\b0\fs24 \cf0 Compares your company\'92s 
\f0\b TRIR (Total Recordable Incident Rate)
\f1\b0  and Average Claim Severity against Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) and NCCI averages for your specific NAICS sector (Manufacturing, Construction, etc.).\

\f1\b0\fs24 \cf0 Utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to scan your corporate 
\f0\b Safety Manual

\f0\b\fs28 \cf0 5. \uc0\u55357 \u56508  Open Claim Reserve Tracker\
\pard\pardeftab720\sa240\partightenfactor0

\f1\b0\fs24 \cf0 Audits the "Future Money" sitting on your books. It flags
